About This Website

This site has been developed by students and staff at RGU who are passionate about supporting student mental health and wellbeing. Learning is so much more manageable when we are feeling good about ourselves. This site is very much a work in progress, and we are hoping that you can give us feedback on what works and what doesn't, what could be added and what could be removed. It is a resource for you after all!

The suggestions and activities here are all supported by peer reviewed research, and the aim is to provide you with the know-how and support to improve or maintain your mental wellbeing and to give you confidence to know when to ask for more help if you need it.

The site is hosted "off" RGU pages so you don't have to log into Moodle to access them. While we do have some links to Moodle content that will require a login, we have no way to identify who you are. This means your visits here are confidential. We are collecting anonymous visit data like how often and when a page or item is accessed. We also have a feedback option if you would like to suggest improvements or tell us something that has worked well. I hope you find the space useful!
