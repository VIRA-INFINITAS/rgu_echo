Hydrate

Water is essential for your body and mind to function at their best

One of the easiest things we can do to support our mental wellbeing is to make sure we are hydrated. It is so easy to forget to drink water, particularly when you're balancing life, work, and study! Drinking water consistently also improves academic outcomes.
