Self-Help Resources

Stress is a normal part of university life, but it's important to manage it effectively:

  • Break tasks into smaller, manageable chunks
  • Practice time management and prioritization
  • Take regular breaks and exercise
  • Talk to someone about how you're feeling
  • Try relaxation techniques like deep breathing

Anxiety can feel overwhelming, but there are ways to manage it:

  • Practice grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 method)
  • Challenge negative thoughts
  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol
  • Consider mindfulness or meditation apps

Good sleep is essential for mental health. Try these tips:

  • Stick to a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
  • Limit naps during the day

Small daily habits can make a big difference:

  • Stay connected with friends and family
  • Engage in activities you enjoy
  • Eat regular, balanced meals
  • Get some sunlight and fresh air daily
  • Practice gratitude - note 3 good things each day
