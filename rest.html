Rest

Rest and sleep are essential for mental and physical recovery

Another easy thing to lose sight of is how much rest you are getting. Sleep is very important in supporting your mental and physical health, and we've put some links to research for that. But as well as sleep, other types of rest are needed - get away from the screens, engage in a hobby, sit quietly for a moment. It can be hard, especially if you've family around, to get space but try to find a time each week just to switch off.

Quick Tip

Create a regular bedtime routine. Turn off screens an hour before sleep, and try to go to bed at the same time each night to help your body develop a healthy sleep rhythm.
