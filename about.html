About RGU Echo

Supporting student mental health and wellbeing

Our Mission

RGU Echo exists to create a supportive, inclusive environment where every Robert Gordon University student can access mental health support and feel heard.

We believe that mental health is just as important as physical health, and no student should face their challenges alone. Through peer support, education, and advocacy, we aim to reduce stigma and promote positive mental wellbeing across campus.

Our student-led initiative works alongside RGU Student Services to ensure that help is accessible, confidential, and tailored to the unique needs of university life.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Confidentiality

Your privacy and trust are paramount. All conversations and interactions are kept strictly confidential.

Inclusivity

We welcome all students regardless of background, identity, or circumstance. Everyone deserves support.

Empathy

We listen without judgment and approach every situation with compassion and understanding.

Empowerment

We provide tools and resources to help you take control of your mental health journey.

What We Do

How we support the RGU student community

Peer Support Groups

Regular meetings where students can share experiences and support each other in a safe, moderated environment.

Awareness Campaigns

We run campaigns throughout the year to reduce stigma and promote mental health awareness on campus.

Educational Workshops

Interactive sessions on stress management, mindfulness, self-care, and other mental health topics.

Signposting Services

We help connect students with professional services, both within RGU and in the wider community.

Want to Get Involved?

We're always looking for passionate students to join our team or participate in our activities.
