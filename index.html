New Tool

GP Note Generator

Struggling to explain how you're feeling to your doctor? Our GP Note Generator helps you organize your thoughts and symptoms.

Answer a few questions about your wellbeing, and we'll create a structured note you can print or save to your phone. This makes it easier to communicate with your GP and ensures you don't forget important details during your appointment.

  • Easy to use - Simple questionnaire format
  • Print or save - Take it with you to your appointment
  • Confidential - Your information stays private
  • Comprehensive - Covers symptoms, duration, and impact
