Move

Physical activity is essential for mental and physical wellbeing

When you're trying to add study into your personal routine it is very easy to reduce your daily step count quite significantly. Make sure you set and keep a regular exercise schedule for your needs. If you are able, try to walk a bit every day, or if walking is not possible, get some fresh air every day. Even 10 minutes outside will give you a boost...
