Our Services

Support tailored to your needs

Peer Support Groups

Join regular group sessions where you can share your experiences with fellow students in a supportive, confidential setting. Led by trained peer supporters.

  • Weekly sessions
  • Small groups (6-8 people)
  • Free for all RGU students

One-to-One Support

Book a confidential chat with one of our trained peer supporters. A safe space to talk about whatever's on your mind without judgment.

  • 45-minute sessions
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Completely confidential

Workshops & Events

Attend our regular workshops on stress management, mindfulness, exam anxiety, and more. Interactive and practical sessions.

  • Monthly workshops
  • Guest speakers
  • Practical tools

Additional Support

Signposting Service

Not sure where to turn? We can help direct you to the right professional services both within RGU and in the wider community, including NHS services, counseling, and crisis support.

Resource Library

Access our curated collection of articles, guides, videos, and apps focused on mental health and wellbeing. Everything from sleep hygiene to managing anxiety.

Awareness Campaigns

Throughout the year, we run campaigns to raise awareness about mental health, reduce stigma, and promote wellbeing across campus. Get involved!

Drop-In Sessions

Can't commit to regular sessions? Visit our drop-in hours in the Student Hub. No appointment needed - just come along for a chat and a cuppa.
